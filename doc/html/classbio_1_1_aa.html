◆ normalize_char()

static char bio::Aa::normalize_char ( char  c)
inlinestatic

Get normalized representation of an amino acid character.

For amino acids, this is the capital single letter IUPAC code, or '*' for a stop. Uppercase amino acid codes and '*' will be returned as-is, lower case amino acid codes will be capitalized, All other characters (invalid amino acids) will be mapped to 0.
